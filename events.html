CCIT Bags Two Plaques and IT Specialist Certification

IBA, Zambales ‚Äî CCIT students bag two plaques of recognition and an IT Specialist Certification at the recent Regional Assembly on Information Technology Education held at Angeles University Foundation, Angeles City Pampanga, last November 4.
